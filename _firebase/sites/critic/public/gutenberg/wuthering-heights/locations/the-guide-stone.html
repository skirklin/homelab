Stone marker at crossroads with carved letters indicating directions: 'W.H.' for Wuthering Heights, 'G.' for Gimmerton village, and 'T.G.' for Thrushcross Grange. Contains a hole filled with snail-shells and pebbles, marking the intersection of the three main locations.

Significance

Symbolic center point representing the intersection of different worlds and choices. Site of nostalgic memories and planned meetings between the younger generation.
